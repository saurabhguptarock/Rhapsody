“Indasonline.com – India’s first interactive “Ind AS e-knowledge platform”.

Entire Ind AS digitalized & online with various analytic tools

The Salient/key features of Indasonline.com are as follows:

  • All standards presented in a very user-friendly and efficient manner.
  • All inter-linkages within and between literature/standards effectively addressed.
  • Inbuilt Definition linkages from Ind AS, Guidance Notes, Schedule III and Companies Act, 2013.
  • Approx. 500 Ind AS/IFRS financial statements of listed and other companies for reference.
  • FAQ’s, EAC opinions and ITFG bulletins arranged in a systematic manner.
  • Other future attractions.
  • The above features make Indasonline.com an intuitive and extremely user-friendly way to read/understand and apply Ind AS (Indian Accounting standards) practically.

This platform can be used for:

  • Drafting Research notes.

  • Drafting Accounting policies and other notes.
  • Resolving Audit queries.
