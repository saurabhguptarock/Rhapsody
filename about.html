Background

Rhapsody was incorporated in 2009 with the objective of creating a high quality Technologies based professional services outfit.

Currently Rhapsody’s core competency is focusing on recent/latest developments in the corporate world pertaining to Accounting, Advisory, Regulatory requirement, corporate law & related research using IT & IT Enabled Services.

Rhapsody currently comprises a team of highly motivated

  • Chartered Accountants
  • Lawyers
  • Company Secretaries
  • Information Technology experts

Our Advantage

  • Technical Capabilities
  • Good experience to handle complex transactions
  • Project management capabilities
  • Cost Effective

Vision

To utilize the technology and developed in house knowledge based application to deliver to high quality of service in the field of

  • Accounting Process Outsourcing
  • Financial Statements Preparation and its consolidation
  • IFRS/Ind AS Advisory
  • Accounting Opinion and Research
  • Merger and Amalgamations (pre and post merger procedures)

Mission

To deliver in the areas of accounting, advisory and Accounting education, high quality, relevant knowledge-based applications, solutions and services under one umbrella.
